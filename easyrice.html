VERY EASY Rice Tutorial

Make delicous and tastly rice like in this random picture i found online, follow simple steps and have the materials(2025 Secret bug 😄)

Materials and Steps

Materials You will need

  • Rice Cooker...
  • Obviously Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • A big bowl

Important steps

  1. Rinse The rice in the big bowl like 4-5 times
  2. Then add the rinsed rice into the Rice cooker
  3. Add water to the rice. The water should be above the rice about 1 inch
  4. Add a pinch of salt into the rice cooker, mix the rice and salt well.
  5. Now close the lid and turn on the rice Cooker
  6. Wait for about 20-30 mins, untill the rice cooker turns off

Now you have it, Easy Rice Technique (2025 Unpatched)
